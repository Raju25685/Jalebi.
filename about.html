Our Story

Sweet Jalebi House has been serving delicious jalebis for generations. Our journey began with a simple passion for creating the perfect jalebi - crispy, sweet, and unforgettable.

What makes us unique is our dedication to maintaining traditional recipes while innovating with new varieties. We take pride in offering three distinct types of jalebis:

  • Classic Red Jalebi - Our signature sweet treat
  • Fiki Jalebi (Sugar-Free) - A healthier alternative
  • Badi Jaleb - Perfect for weddings and special occasions
